📝 How to File a Complaint

1️⃣

Visit the Portal

Click the button above and choose "Report Other Cyber Crime".

2️⃣

Create/Login Account

Register using your mobile number and verify with OTP.

3️⃣

Submit & Track

After submission, you will receive a complaint ID to track status.
